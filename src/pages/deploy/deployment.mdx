# 🚀 How to Deploy Your Project on Vercel

Follow these super easy steps to deploy your project on Vercel!

## 1️⃣ Create a Vercel Account

- Go to [Vercel](https://vercel.com) and **sign up**.
- You can use your **GitHub**, **GitLab**, or **Bitbucket** account!

## 2️⃣ Connect Your Project

- After signing in, click the **New Project** button.
- Choose the project from your **GitHub**, **GitLab**, or **Bitbucket** repository.

## 3️⃣ Configure Your Project

- Select your project’s **framework** (Next.js, React, etc.).
- Review the settings (don’t worry, they’re already perfect by default 😉).

## 4️⃣ Deploy!

- Click the **Deploy** button and wait a few seconds. 🎉
- Vercel will give you a **live URL** where you can see your project online!

## 5️⃣ Done! 🎉

Now your project is live on the internet! Share your link with friends, and they can see what you’ve built! 🌍
